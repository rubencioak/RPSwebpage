Welcome to my webpage, I am a job market candidate in the International Doctorate in Economic Analysis (IDEA) programme at Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB) and the Barcelona School of Economics (BSE), currently working under the supervision of Joan Llull.

My research interests are focused on Labour Markets and (Structural) Microeconometrics. Particularly, I am interested in working unions and collective agreements using Social Security data from Spain.

At present I am researching how colletively agreed minimum wages decided by a minority of agents - probably in their interests - moulds the decision of firms to hire and workers to be up for hiring, which in turn allows to study traditional measures in the labour market such as unemployment and wage distributions.
